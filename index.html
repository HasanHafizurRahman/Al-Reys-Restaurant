About Alreys Somali Restaurant

At Alreys, we bring you the true taste of Somali and Mediterranean cuisine, served with warmth and hospitality. Located in the heart of town, our restaurant offers a comfortable and stylish dining space with ample seating capacity, making it perfect for families, friends, and groups of all sizes.

Whether you’re looking for a relaxed lunch, a memorable dinner, or a place to celebrate a special occasion, Alreys is here to make your experience unforgettable. We also provide convenient on-site parking, ensuring your visit is as stress-free as possible.

Come and savour authentic dishes prepared with the finest ingredients, blending rich Somali flavours with the freshness of Mediterranean cooking.
