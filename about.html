About Al Reys

Bringing authentic Somali & Mediterranean flavours to the heart of town — served with warmth, family recipes and fresh ingredients.

See Menu

Our Story

At Al Reys, we bring you the true taste of Somali and Mediterranean cuisine, served with warmth and hospitality. Located in the heart of town, our restaurant offers a comfortable and stylish dining space with ample seating capacity — perfect for families, friends and groups of all sizes.

Whether you’re after a relaxed lunch, a memorable dinner, or a place to celebrate a special occasion, Al Reys aims to make your experience unforgettable. We use the finest ingredients and traditional spice blends to blend rich Somali flavours with Mediterranean freshness.

What we serve

Our menu is a celebration of flavour, culture and tradition. Everything is freshly prepared so you can enjoy an authentic taste of Somali heritage alongside Mediterranean favourites.

  • Starters — Light bites and appetisers to awaken your appetite.
  • Main courses — Hearty Somali dishes, grills, seafood, vegetarian options and Mediterranean classics.
  • Sides & salads — Freshly made to complement your meal.
  • Desserts & drinks — Sweet endings and refreshing beverages.

Private hire & events

Looking for a place to celebrate? Al Reys is great for birthdays, family gatherings, business dinners and private events. With generous seating and on-site parking, we can host your guests comfortably and help you plan the menu.

Book in advance to secure your date — contact our events team and we’ll take care of the rest.
